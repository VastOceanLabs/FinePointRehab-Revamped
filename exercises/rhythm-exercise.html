How to play

Watch the pads light up in a sequence, then repeat the pattern. Each round adds one more step. Keep a long streak and finish quickly to boost your score.

  • Tap 1–9 (or press keys 1–9 on desktop).
  • Use Pause to take a break; Exit returns you to this screen.
  • Session ends when the timer reaches 0.

Gamification: Score +10 per correct tap, −5 on mistakes; Personal Best is saved locally.
