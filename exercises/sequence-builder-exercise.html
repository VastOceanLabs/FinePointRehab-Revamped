🧩 How to Play

Simple Controls: Click/tap pieces to select them, then click/tap empty slots to place them. Build the pattern shown at the top!

Goal: Complete as many sequences as possible within the time limit with high accuracy to improve your executive function and sequential processing abilities.

Choose what kind of sequences you'll practice with:
  • Pattern: Geometric shapes and symbols in repeating patterns
  • Assembly: Tools and building components in logical order
  • Numerical: Numbers and math symbols in mathematical sequences
  • Spatial: Directional arrows and movement patterns
Controls how many steps each sequence has and how many distractor pieces appear:
  • Easy: 3-4 steps per sequence, 2 distractor pieces
  • Medium: 5-6 steps per sequence, 4 distractor pieces
  • Hard: 7-8 steps per sequence, 6 distractor pieces
Choose how long you can see the target pattern:
  • Always: Target pattern stays visible (easier, focuses on sequencing)
  • Initially: Pattern shows for 5 seconds, then disappears (memory + sequencing)
  • Never: Pattern shows for 2 seconds only (hardest, pure memory challenge)
