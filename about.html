Our Story

Fine Point Rehab began when our founders, two Occupational Therapists with over 25 years of combined experience, recognized a critical gap in rehabilitation care. Many patients struggled to maintain their exercise regimens at home, with traditional paper handouts often lost, forgotten, or abandoned due to lack of engagement.

We saw patients make remarkable progress in clinical sessions, only to struggle with home practice. We knew there had to be a better way to bridge this gap.

The breakthrough came when we began exploring how digital experiences could transform traditional rehabilitation movements into engaging, interactive exercises. By partnering with technology experts and fellow rehabilitation specialists, we developed a suite of exercises that maintained therapeutic integrity while dramatically improving patient engagement.

What started as a simple concept has grown into a comprehensive platform that serves thousands of users worldwide, proving that the intersection of clinical expertise and digital innovation can create powerful tools for recovery.

Research & Evidence

Scientific Foundation

Fine Point Rehab is committed to evidence-based practice. Our exercises are designed based on established principles of motor learning, neuroplasticity, and rehabilitation science.

We continuously review current research in neurorehabilitation, occupational therapy, and digital therapeutics to ensure our exercises align with best practices and emerging evidence.

Proven Engagement

Recent studies have shown that gamified rehabilitation exercises can significantly improve adherence to home exercise programs, with corresponding improvements in functional outcomes.

Our own internal data suggests that users engage with our digital exercises 3-4 times more frequently than with traditional paper handouts, leading to more consistent practice and better therapeutic outcomes.
